This project currently ships as a single-site dashboard. Multi-site / commercial support below is a direction, not a shipped feature.

The same monitoring, at a larger scale

The underlying model — readings, alerts, billing estimates, a swappable data-source driver — doesn't change for a larger installation. A commercial rooftop or ground-mount system produces the same kinds of numbers, just bigger.

What would need to change

Supporting multiple sites under one login, per-site tariff configuration, and role-based access for a facilities team are the natural next steps if this project grows past a single home installation.
