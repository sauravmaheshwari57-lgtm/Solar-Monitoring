Built around a single rooftop system

Whether you've got a small residential inverter or a hybrid system with battery storage, the dashboard tracks the same core numbers homeowners care about: how much you generated today, how much you used, how much you sent back to the grid, and what that's likely to cost — or save — you this month.

What you get

A live view of generation and consumption, a battery charge meter, an estimated monthly bill based on your own tariff and feed-in rate, and alerts if the inverter faults or output drops unexpectedly.

Getting started

Sign up, log in, and the dashboard starts populating immediately with simulated readings so you can see how it works. Swap in your real inverter's data whenever you're ready.
